Sam's Kitchen was founded by three friends—one from South India, one from North India, and one from Pakistan—who met at university. They shared a love for food and culture, inspiring them to create a restaurant that celebrates their rich heritage. From Hyderabadi biryanis to roti cutter chicken to spicy Pakistani kebabs, Sam's Kitchen combines the best flavors from their homelands to bring you a unique dining experience.

Sri Sasmi Polu

Bringing the warmth of South India to Sam's Kitchen, she specializes in creating aromatic Hyderabadi biryanis, flavorful sambars, and crispy dosas. Her dishes are infused with the spices and traditions of her homeland, offering a true taste of South India.

Anchal Singh

From buttery naans to the irresistible roti butter chicken, our North Indian founder combines bold flavors with her family's cherished recipes. Her passion for creating rich and satisfying meals brings the heart of North Indian cuisine to every table.

Maliha Aziz

Specializing in the smoky, savory delights of Pakistani cuisine, our founder crafts succulent kebabs, spicy curries, and perfectly charred naans. Her dishes bring the vibrant flavors of Pakistan to life, making every bite unforgettable.

Our Values

At Sam's Kitchen, we believe in celebrating diversity, fostering cultural unity, and sharing the joy of great food. We are committed to quality, authenticity, and innovation, ensuring every meal is a celebration of flavor and community.
